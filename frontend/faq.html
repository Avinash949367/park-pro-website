ParkPro - Frequently Asked Questions

Top 10 FAQs

1. What is ParkPro?
ParkPro is a smart parking management system designed to simplify parking for both drivers and parking facility operators.
2. How do I book a parking spot using ParkPro?
You can book a parking spot through our website or mobile app by selecting your location, date, and time, then confirming availability.
3. Can I cancel or change my booking?
Yes, you can cancel or modify bookings up to 1 hour before the reserved time via your account dashboard.
4. What payment methods are accepted?
ParkPro accepts all major credit/debit cards, digital wallets like Apple Pay and Google Pay, and PayPal.
5. Is ParkPro available in my city?
ParkPro operates in select cities which keep expanding. Check our website or app for the latest availability.
6. How do I access a booked parking spot?
Upon arrival, show your booking confirmation QR code at the entry gate, or use the ParkPro app to open automated gates.
7. Does ParkPro offer monthly parking plans?
Yes, we offer monthly and longer-term subscriptions for frequent parkers with discounted rates.
8. How secure is my payment information?
We use industry-leading encryption and comply with PCI DSS standards to ensure the security of your data.
9. What should I do if I lose my booking confirmation?
You can retrieve your booking details by logging into your ParkPro account or by contacting our support team.
10. How can I contact ParkPro customer support?
You can reach out to us via the contact form below, email, or our 24/7 support hotline.
