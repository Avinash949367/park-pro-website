About ParkPro

Find parking solution for all type of vehicles with Modern technology

Our Story

Built in 2025, ParkPro began as a group of Students with a big vision: to solve the growing parking crisis in urban areas. Our team came together with a shared mission to create intelligent parking solutions that reduce congestion, lower emissions, and improve quality of life in cities.

What started as a university project has now grown into a leading parking management platform serving over 50 cities worldwide. Our patented sensor technology and AI-powered prediction algorithms help drivers find parking faster while giving cities valuable data to optimize their parking infrastructure.

Today, we continue to innovate with new features AI Assisstent, infrastructure GPS and more to create seamless mobility experiences.

Our Mission

To transform urban mobility by developing intelligent parking solutions that reduce congestion, lower emissions, and make cities more livable through cutting-edge technology and data-driven insights.

Our Vision

To create a world where finding parking is effortless, cities are less congested, and transportation systems are seamlessly integrated - making urban living more sustainable and enjoyable for everyone.
