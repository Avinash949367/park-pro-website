Documentation Process

You'll receive an email with a link to submit the required documents.

Required Documents:

  • Land/Lease documents
  • Pictures of your existing parking station
  • Partnership agreement (to partner with ParkPro)
  • Personal Documents: Aadhar card, passport-size photo
