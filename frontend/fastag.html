ParkPro FASTag Information

üè∑Ô∏è What is FASTag?

FASTag is a Radio Frequency Identification (RFID) passive tag used for automatic toll payments directly from your linked prepaid, savings, or current bank account. It is affixed to the windscreen of your vehicle, allowing you to drive through toll plazas without stopping, saving time and fuel.

Important: FASTag is vehicle-specific and once installed, it cannot be transferred to another vehicle. If linked to a prepaid account, it requires timely recharges to remain active.

üß≠ How to Use FASTag

Using FASTag is simple:

  1. Affix the FASTag on your vehicle's front windscreen (from inside).
  2. Link it to ParkPro wallet (QR provided by us after registration with linked account)
  3. As you pass through any ParkPro-enabled toll plaza, the system will:
    • Automatically detect your FASTag
    • Deduct the applicable toll amount and detect amount and give access to the entry
    • Send you an SMS confirmation of the transaction

You don't need to stop or handle cash. Just drive through the FASTag lane!

üõí How to Get FASTag

You can buy FASTag from the ParkPro website. The process includes:

  • Provide your vehicle registration details and KYC documents
  • The new wallet will be created
  • Once issued, your tag will be activated and ready for use

üí° You can recharge your FASTag online via UPI, Net Banking, Credit/Debit Card, NEFT or RTGS.

üöÄ How It Works: ParkPro FASTag Transaction Flow

1

Vehicle Approaches Entry Gate

The vehicle enters the ParkPro Smart Parking Zone.

The QR scanner camera is installed at the entry gate.

2

QR Code Presented

The driver presents a QR code from:

  • The ParkPro mobile app, or
  • A pre-booked pass, or
  • The Fastag QR
3

QR Code Scanning & Validation

The system scans the QR code and instantly sends the data to the ParkPro Cloud Server.

ParkPro validates:

  • QR Code authenticity (not expired, not reused)
  • Booking or subscription status
  • Linked vehicle number (if required)
  • Parking zone match (is the QR valid at this entry point?)
4

Vehicle Identity Mapping

If QR is linked with vehicle registration:

  • The system fetches vehicle class (2-wheeler, 4-wheeler, etc.)
  • Verifies against the parking rules or assigned slot type

(Optional step for enhanced verification)

5

Entry Authorization

If validation is successful:

  • ParkPro records entry time, QR code ID, and vehicle details
  • A temporary entry token is generated and stored in the database
6

Barrier Control

The entry gate barrier lifts automatically.

LED display or audio prompts can show:

  • "Welcome [User/Vehicle]"
  • "Slot assigned: A12" (if auto slot allocation is enabled)
7

Slot Allocation (AI + IoT)
