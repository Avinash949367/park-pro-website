About FastTag

What is FastTag?

FastTag is an electronic toll collection system in India implemented by the National Highway Authority of India (NHAI). It uses Radio Frequency Identification (RFID) technology to enable automatic deduction of toll charges without stopping vehicles at toll plazas.

Key Benefits

  • Saves time: No need to stop at toll plazas
  • Reduces traffic: FastTag lanes are 3x faster than cash lanes
  • Cashless payments: Automatic deduction from linked account
  • Discounts: 2.5% cashback on toll payments (NHAI FastTag)
  • 24/7 Online management: Check balance and recharge anytime
  • Environment friendly: Reduces fuel consumption and pollution

How to Use FastTag

At Toll Plazas:

  • Look for dedicated FastTag lanes (usually marked with green signs)
  • Reduce speed to under 25 km/h when approaching toll plaza
  • RFID scanner will automatically detect your FastTag
  • Toll amount will be deducted from your prepaid balance
  • Barrier will open automatically without stopping

Proper Placement:

  • Affix the tag on your vehicle's windshield as per instructions
  • For cars: Top center position on the inside of the windshield
  • For trucks: Middle of the windshield on driver's side
  • Don't cover the tag with any objects or stickers
