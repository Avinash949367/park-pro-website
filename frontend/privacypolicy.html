Privacy Policy

ParkPro Plus Parking Solutions

Effective Date:

Acknowledgment: By using ParkPro Plus, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy, which outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect and process the following categories of information:

  • Personal Identification Data: Name, contact number, email address, billing details
  • Account Information: Username, encrypted password, and preferences
  • Vehicle Details: License plate number, make, model, and color
  • Location Data: Real-time GPS data for identifying nearby parking facilities
  • Usage Data: Booking history, search preferences, and interactions within our platform
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers, and network details

2. Purpose of Data Collection

We collect and use your information to:

  • Process bookings and manage your account
  • Provide accurate parking availability and navigation services
  • Send booking confirmations, alerts, and transaction receipts
  • Enhance platform performance and user experience
  • Ensure security, prevent fraudulent activities, and resolve technical issues
  • Share relevant offers and service updates (with your consent)

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

Your information is never sold. It may be shared only in the following situations:

  • Service Providers & Partners: To facilitate bookings, payment processing, and customer support
  • Legal Requirements: When disclosure is necessary to comply with laws, regulations, or legal processes
  • Corporate Transactions: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset transfer involving ParkPro Plus

4. Data Security & Retention

  • We implement strict technical and organizational measures to safeguard your information
  • Data is encrypted in storage and during transmission
  • Access to personal data is restricted to authorized personnel only
  • We retain data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Improve platform functionality and user navigation
  • Remember your preferences for future visits
  • Analyze usage trends to enhance our services

You may disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect functionality.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Correct or update inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data, subject to legal obligations
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications at any time

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on our website, and the "Effective Date" will be updated accordingly.
